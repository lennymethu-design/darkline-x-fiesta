Common Questions

FAQ

How do I buy tickets? +
Go to the Events page and click Pay with M-Pesa on your preferred ticket. You'll receive an STK Push on your phone to complete payment instantly.
Can I buy tickets at the gate? +
Yes, tickets will be available at the gate via M-Pesa. However we recommend buying in advance as tickets are limited and may sell out.
What's included in VIP? +
VIP includes panoramic rooftop access, DJ booth proximity, reserved seating, and exclusive pool table access. Full-Pack VIP adds tournament access on top of everything.
Is there parking at Arena One? +
Yes, Arena One at Valley Arcade has parking available. We recommend arriving early as spaces fill up fast for events of this size.
Can I get a refund? +
Tickets are non-refundable but are transferable. If you can't make it, you can transfer your ticket to someone else. Contact us for help.
How do I book for a group? +
For groups of 5 or more, use the contact form above or WhatsApp us directly. We offer group packages and can arrange reserved areas for large groups.
