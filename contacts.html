Common Questions

FAQ

Where do I buy tickets?
Tickets are available on Sokonet at darkfiesta.soko.net. Also available at the gate on the day via M-Pesa. Contact us for group bookings of 5 or more.
What's included in each ticket?
Spectator tickets cover the tournament. Regular tickets cover the afterparty. VIP and Full-Pack include rooftop access, reserved seating, and DJ booth proximity.
Can I attend just the tournament or just the party?
Yes! The Fiesta Spectator ticket is for the tournament only (12PM–7PM). The Darkline Regular ticket is for the afterparty only (8PM onwards).
Is there parking at the venue?
Arena One at Valley Arcade has parking available. We recommend arriving early as spaces are limited, especially for evening sessions.
How do I register a team for the tournament?
Team registrations require a Fiesta Teams bundle ticket. Buy on Sokonet or send us a message using the form above.
Are there sponsorship opportunities?
Yes! We offer brand visibility packages for both the tournament and the afterparty. Get in touch via the contact form and we'll send you our sponsorship deck.
