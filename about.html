Where It Started

BUILT FOR
THE CITY.

Darkline Party started with a simple idea — Nairobi deserves better nights. Not just parties, but experiences. Rooftop sessions, curated sounds, and a crowd that actually cares about the vibe.

Footwork Fiesta came from the same energy — grassroots football with a competitive edge and a community at its core. Real teams. Real stakes. Real Nairobi.

In 2026, the two worlds collide for the first time. One venue. One day. Two experiences that define what this city is capable of.
